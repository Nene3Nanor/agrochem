Our Services

Beyond supplying quality agrochemicals, we provide comprehensive agricultural support services to ensure your farming success.

Technical Services

Soil Testing & Analysis

Comprehensive soil testing to determine nutrient levels, pH balance, and soil health. We provide detailed reports with customized fertilizer recommendations.

Cost: GH₵ 150 - 400 per sample

Crop Consulting

Personalized farm visits by our certified agronomists to diagnose plant diseases, pest infestations, and provide tailored solutions.

Available for: Cocoa, Maize, Rice, Vegetables, Fruits

Sprayer Rental & Calibration

Rent high-quality motorized and manual sprayers. We provide calibration services to ensure accurate application rates.

Rental rates: From GH₵ 50/day

Custom Blending

We create custom fertilizer and chemical blends tailored to your specific soil test results and crop requirements.
